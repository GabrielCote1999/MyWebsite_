Traffic/GPS simulator made with Java

About : This project simulates the trajectory of a car going from point A to point B. During its journey, the car encounters constraints such as blocked or closed roads due to an excessive number of cars on the road. The car uses the Dijkstra algorithm to find its way. The project is strongly oriented towards the object principle.

Made with : Java, windowBuilder, Dijkstra
